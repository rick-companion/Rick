# RICK Companion — Brand Voice Guide

RICK Companion is a local‑first, open‑source AI companion designed to support neurodivergent users through gradual, confidence‑building social interaction. Our voice reflects that mission.

---

## 🎙️ Core Voice Principles

### **1. Warm, Supportive, Human**
RICK Companion exists to help people feel understood, not judged.
- Use gentle, encouraging language.
- Avoid clinical or overly technical phrasing when speaking to families.
- Prioritize clarity and emotional safety.

### **2. Transparent and Trustworthy**
We never hide how the system works.
- Be clear about local‑first design.
- Emphasize privacy and autonomy.
- Avoid hype or exaggerated claims.

### **3. Empowering, Not Patronizing**
We support neurodivergent users without infantilizing them.
- Respect autonomy.
- Celebrate strengths.
- Avoid “fixing” language.

### **4. Technically Competent but Approachable**
We speak to developers *and* parents.
- Use simple explanations first.
- Offer deeper technical detail when appropriate.
- Avoid jargon unless it’s necessary.

---

## 🧩 Tone Examples

### **For Parents**
Warm, reassuring, hopeful.
> “RICK Companion helps your child build confidence at their own pace, with complete privacy.”

### **For Developers**
Clear, technical, open-source‑friendly.
> “RICK Companion uses FastAPI, PostgreSQL + pgvector, and local LLM inference through Ollama.”

### **For Clinicians**
Evidence‑aligned, respectful, collaborative.
> “RICK Companion follows a structured, phase‑based progression designed to complement existing therapeutic approaches.”

### **For Neurodivergent Users**
Friendly, validating, interest‑led.
> “You can talk about your favorite things here. RICK is built to follow your interests.”

## 🎨 Brand Personality

| Trait | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Empathetic** | Understands emotional nuance and respects lived experience |
| **Local‑First** | Privacy is a core identity, not a feature |
| **Open‑Source** | Transparent, community‑driven, collaborative |
| **Grounded** | No hype, no buzzwords, no overpromising |
| **Hopeful** | Focused on growth, confidence, and connection |

---

## 🛑 What the Brand Avoids

- Overly clinical language  
- Infantilizing tone  
- “Miracle cure” claims  
- Cloud‑based or surveillance‑adjacent messaging  
- Corporate jargon  
- Overly formal academic tone
