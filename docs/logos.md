# RICK Companion — Logo Concepts & Usage Guide

This document outlines logo ideas, usage rules, and brand consistency guidelines.

---

## 🧩 Logo Concept Themes

### **1. Friendly Robot Head**
A simple, approachable robot face with soft edges.  
Represents companionship and safety.

### **2. Speech Bubble + Heart**
Symbolizes communication, emotional support, and connection.

### **3. Shield + Chat Icon**
Emphasizes privacy and protection.

### **4. “R” Monogram**
Minimalist, modern, easy to scale.  
Can be used as an app icon.

---

## 🎨 Logo Usage Guidelines

### **Primary Logo**
- Full “RICK Companion” text  
- Horizontal layout  
- Used for banners, website headers, and documentation  

### **Secondary Logo**
- Icon‑only version  
- Used for app icons, favicons, and small spaces  

### **Monochrome Version**
- For dark mode  
- For print  
- For embroidery or merchandise  

---

## 📏 Clear Space Rules

Leave at least **1× the height of the “R”** around the logo on all sides.

---

## 🛑 Incorrect Usage

- Do not stretch or distort  
- Do not change colors outside the palette  
- Do not add drop shadows or outlines  
- Do not place on busy backgrounds
