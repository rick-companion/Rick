# RICK Companion — Platform Strategy

Each platform serves a different purpose. This guide explains how to use them effectively without burning out.

---

## 🐦 X (Twitter)
**Audience:** Developers, influencers, tech journalists  
**Purpose:** Updates, outreach, credibility  
**Content:** Screenshots, short posts, announcements  
**Cadence:** 3–5 posts/week  

---

## 🎵 TikTok
**Audience:** Parents, neurodivergent adults, clinicians  
**Purpose:** Emotional storytelling, demos  
**Content:** Short videos, captions, voiceovers  
**Cadence:** 2–3 posts/week  

---

## ▶️ YouTube
**Audience:** Developers, researchers, tech community  
**Purpose:** Devlogs, deep dives, demos  
**Content:** 3–10 minute videos  
**Cadence:** 1 video/week  

---

## 🧩 Reddit
**Audience:** Homelab, open-source, autism communities  
**Purpose:** Feedback, testing, visibility  
**Content:** Long-form posts, architecture breakdowns  
**Cadence:** 1–2 posts/week  

---

## 📘 Facebook
**Audience:** Parents, educators, clinicians  
**Purpose:** Trust-building, updates  
**Content:** Simple posts, links, images  
**Cadence:** 1–2 posts/week  

---

## 📸 Instagram
**Audience:** General public  
**Purpose:** Visual storytelling  
**Content:** Screenshots, quotes, short clips  
**Cadence:** 1–2 posts/week  

---

## Summary

| Platform | Purpose | Cadence |
|----------|----------|----------|
| X | Updates, outreach | 3–5/week |
| TikTok | Storytelling, demos | 2–3/week |
| YouTube | Devlogs, deep dives | 1/week |
| Reddit | Feedback, testing | 1–2/week |
| Facebook | Parent outreach | 1–2/week |
| Instagram | Visual storytelling | 1–2/week |
